General Instruction


1.Team Size:1-2 members.


Line Follower Race


Rules and Regulations


1.The size of the robot shall not exceed 20cm (length) x 20cm (width) x 20cm (height).

2.Robot must move autonomously and start with a push a button. Any wireless/wired remote control is not allowed.

3.Robot will be placed in one end of the track and the time to reach the finish line is measured.

4.The robot which reaches the finish line faster is considered as winner.

5.The electric voltage anywhere in the machine must not exceed 12V DC at all times for each robot. .


Maze Solver


Rules and Regulations


1.MicroMouse shall not jump over, fly over, climb, scratch, cut, burn, mark, damage, or destroy the walls of the maze.

2.Size of micromouse shall not exceed 17cm(height)*17cm(width).

3.Micromouse should not exceed maximum weight of 2kg.

4.The Micromouse must be controlled autonomously with no human aid. A MicroMouse shall not use an energy source employing a combustion process.

5.The robot must be powered by a power source such as a battery fixed on the robot. It cannot be powered by a stationary power source connected to the robot by a cord.

6.There will be two penalties, one is a search penalty that tells about how much time is being spent on the maze to start its new run and the other penalty is a touch penalty that tells about how many times the micro-mouse touched the walls.

7.A runtime is noted after every run of a mouse,covering the distance from the starting position to the destination.
